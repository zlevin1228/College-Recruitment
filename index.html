UNIVERSITY OF VIRGINIA

Charlottesville, Virginia

"To Be Good And Great In All We Do"

The University of Virginia (UVA) is a prestigious public research university located in Charlottesville, Virginia. Founded in 1819 by Thomas Jefferson, it stands out for its distinctive Jeffersonian architecture and deep historical significance. Jefferson not only designed much of the campus, including the iconic Rotunda, but also envisioned a university focused on fostering knowledge, critical thinking, and public service.
